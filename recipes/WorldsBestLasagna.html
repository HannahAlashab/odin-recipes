World's Best Lasagna

This is the best lasagna recipe ever! It's easy to make and delicious. I keep this one handy whenever I feel like making a hearty meal.


Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (8 oz each) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 sheets frozen lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef and Italian sausage in a large skillet over medium heat until browned.
  3. Drain the excess fat and return the meat to the skillet.
  4. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, sugar, salt, pepper, oregano, and basil.
  6. Simmer the sauce for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  8. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and egg.
  9. Spread a layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Place a layer of noodles on top of the sauce.
  11. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  12. Top with a layer of sauce and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  13. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of sauce and Parmesan cheese.
  14. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
